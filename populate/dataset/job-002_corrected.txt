Fifty two year old female with type two diabetes mellitus presents for routine follow up. Blood glucose levels have been suboptimal over the past month averaging one hundred eighty milligrams per deciliter. Patient reports adherence to metformin five hundred milligrams twice daily but admits dietary indiscretion during holidays. HbA1c measured at eight point two percent up from seven point one three months ago. Fundoscopic examination shows no diabetic retinopathy. Foot examination reveals intact sensation bilaterally with palpable pedal pulses. Kidney function tests within normal limits with creatinine zero point nine. Increased metformin to one thousand milligrams twice daily. Referred to dietician for nutritional counseling. Patient educated on importance of carbohydrate counting and regular exercise. Recheck HbA1c in three months.
